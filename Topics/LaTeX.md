#### Formatting 

[Command name][optional arguments]{required arguments}

#### Commands:
- twocolumn
- titlepage
- legno - equation on left side
- flegn - left align equations vs centre
- twoside - print on both sides
- openright - w/ twoside chapters begin on right hand page
- landscape
